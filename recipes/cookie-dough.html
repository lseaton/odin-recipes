Cookie Dough

Description

Sometimes I want cookies but don't have the patience for the time required to cook them. This delicious egg-free recipe is here to save the day in situations such as these. It makes a single serving.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp melted unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp milk
  • 1/8 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp mini chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Melt the butter.
  2. Whisk the butter and sugars and salt together.
  3. Stir in milk and vanilla.
  4. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips.
  5. Enjoy!
